Honey Garlic Chicken

One-pot, slow cooker honey garlic chicken is an easy and delicious meal that can be thrown in a pot and left.

Ingredients

  • Chicken -- Boneless and Skinless
  • Honey
  • Garlic (Minced)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chili Paste
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Corn Starch
  • Jasmine Rice
  • Vegetable of choice

Directions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the Honey, Garlic, Soy Sauce, Tomato Paste, Chili Paste, and Rice Vinegar.
  2. Add chicken and sauce to the slow cooker
  3. Cook the mixture on LOW for 4 to 5 hours
  4. Cook the Rice and Vegetables when it is time to serve
  5. Remove and shred Chicken
  6. Add Corn Starch to thicken the sauce
  7. Enjoy!
